About The Event

About The Event

A connotation of flamboyant manner and reckless courage, a word derived from the helmet-plume worn by men of the Early Modern period in the battlefield, “Panache”. The Panache started five years ago, as a celebration of the spirit of one’s creativity and to induce in the students a sense of interest towards the English language but has spread its wings beyond its aspirations. In a world full of cultural and technical college fests, The Panache has managed to stand out, has maintained its rich variety across its event line-up ranging from Graffiti, Oratory Events, Creative Writing, and even Scavenger Hunts! We, from the B.P. Poddar Institute of Management and Technology, Kolkata, bring to you the 5th edition of this extravaganza on the 7th & 8th of April, 2020.

Reminiscence

Back in '15, as a scared teenager who just came to college, getting Panache as the first fest of college life and winning an event called 'Word Cracker' was overwhelming. Panache '17 was a huge success, organizing, participating as well as winning (yes, ECE, we need the trophy this year as well!) is probably one of the biggest chapter of my college diaries. It not only strengthened our language skills through fun & games but also strengthened the bonds we shared with seniors, juniors and batchmates. As I look today and see the tremendous effort the team is putting for this year's Panache, I know the standard is going to be way higher than what we set back in '17. Loads of love and luck!

Medha Bhattacharya(2019 Batch)

Qualifying prelims gave a sense of confidence but what really stayed as a memory was the cheer of the audience as my viewpoint crashed down hard on the opposite team. The aura when you feel a crowd listening to your stance gave an experience rejuvenating my voice. The event not only made me feel that I too hold a ground but also gave an opportunity to make my existence be felt in the crowd of many.

Shabina Arfa(2020 Batch)

Despite having organized several other events in my 4 years of college life, Panache will perhaps always be special. To this year's organizers, wait till the fest almost becomes your baby, till you're burnt and broken with all the work but still keep going because that's the thrill of it all! :) So yes, kids, Be something greater Go make a legacy.

Shreya Chatterjee(2019 Batch)
